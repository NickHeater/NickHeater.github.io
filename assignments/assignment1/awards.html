Awards

Young Thug has been nominated for and won many awards so far in his career. This includes a Grammy win and a few MTV Video Music Award nominations. Young Thug has 2 projects that have went gold and 2 singles that went double platinum. He has also accumulated more than 21 million monthly listeners on Spotify.
